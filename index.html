CALL FOR SPEAKERS

Have a story to tell? Come share your knowledge and experience with functional programming.

flatMap(Oslo) will include 40 minute talks. We hope you want to join us in making flatMap(Oslo) an experience out of the ordinary.

flatMap's main focus is on Scala and other functional programming languages on the Java Virtual Machine. However, this shouldn't keep you from suggesting something different.



We are accepting technical talks and workshops until April 3rd.

Submitted proposals are continually evaluated and accepted so don't wait until the last minute. We may be fully booked by then.


Send your proposal to flatmap@arktekk.no

LOCATION

flatMap(Oslo) 2016 will be held at Teknologihuset - House of Communities.

Teknologihuset is situated in Pilestredet 56, not far from the city center in Oslo.

flatMap(Oslo) - A functional programming conference, with focus on Scala and the Java Virtual Machine.

On the 27th and 28th of April 2015, a group of enthusiasts organised the fourth flatMap(Oslo) conference. It was 2 amazing days. 100 attendees gathered for technical talks, workshops, discussions and craft beer. Feedback was great, and in 2016 we gather again for the 5th annual flatMap(Oslo). The goal for this year is the same. Small and intimate, but with high ambitions.
